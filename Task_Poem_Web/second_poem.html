Serenade of Silence

In dawn's embrace, where dreams arise,

Serenity whispers in muted sighs.

Sunrise paints tales on the canvas of day,

Mysteries woven in the morning's ballet.

Each sunbeam a stanza, soft and sublime,

Resonating through the corridors of time.

Nature's ballad, hummed by the stream,

A lullaby whispered in the moonbeam.
