A Whispers' Dance

In twilight's hush, where shadows play,

Whispers dance in the softest way.

Stars above, their tales unfold,

Secrets shared in the moonlight's hold.

Each word a note, a gentle rhyme,

Echoing through the corridors of time.

Nature's verses, sung by the breeze,

A symphony among the ancient trees.
